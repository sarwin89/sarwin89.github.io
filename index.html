About
Me

I am a curious builder who likes mixing analysis with imagination.

I am Sarwin Chandran, and this site is where I document both the work and the thinking behind it. Some days it is simulation-heavy, some days it is sketchbook-heavy, and sometimes both happen in the same night.

Instead of splitting my interests into separate identities, I wanted one honest home that shows how they feed each other: technical depth, visual storytelling, and reflective writing.

  • Focus: computational physics, visual storytelling, and creative experimentation
  • Current mission: ship projects consistently and document each phase in public
  • Home base: this portfolio plus blog on GitHub Pages
