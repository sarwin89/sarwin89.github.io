Selected
Pieces

A rotating notebook of lines I revisit, rewrite, and occasionally let stay unfinished.

Orbit

I keep circling old questions,
hoping one revolution later
they turn into answers.

After Midnight

Streetlights become constellations
when the city finally slows down
enough to hear your own pulse.

Graph Paper

I plotted silence as a function,
then noticed every axis tilted
towards a forgotten name.

Simulation Log

Some variables are numbers.
Some variables are courage.
Both need tuning.

Window Seat

Clouds looked like erased equations,
and still the sky solved itself before we landed.

Draft Zero

The first line never arrives finished.
It arrives breathing, waiting for weather.
