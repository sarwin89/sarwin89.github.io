Process notes

The most useful art notes are usually not technical tutorials. They are records of what decision changed the image.

I want the archive to remember what actually helped: where lighting got cleaner, where the pose loosened up, or where I finally stopped overworking a face.

That makes the page useful later. It turns the archive from a wall of images into a record of practice.

Questions I keep asking
Does the silhouette read fast?
If not, no amount of rendering will save the image.
Is color doing real work?
Palette should push mood or focus, not sit there decoratively.
Did the final pass help?
Polish is only useful when it strengthens the image rather than flattening it.
